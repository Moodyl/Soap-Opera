The following guide is going to teach you how to wash your hands. Proper handwashing is one of the most important things you can do to prevent the spread of germs and stay healthy. It's a simple yet effective way to protect yourself and those around you from illness.

In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of proper handwashing technique and provide you with tips to make sure you're washing your hands thoroughly and effectively.

1st

step
Wet hands, apply soap and spread it across both your hands

2nd

step
Scrub between your fingers from each side (front and back)
and the inside of your palms together

3rd

step
Scrub each thumb with your whole hand and each palm with
your fingertips, rinse away the soap

4th

step
Dry your hands with a towel and close the faucet with the former,
admire your shiny clean hands!

Iconography of cleanliness

Handwashing has been a symbol of purity and hygiene throughout history, depicted in various forms of iconography. It was ritualistic in ancient civilizations, religious in the Middle Ages, and promoted for public health during the 19th century. Today, it remains crucial for preventing the spread of illness and is still represented in art and media as a symbol of cleanliness.
